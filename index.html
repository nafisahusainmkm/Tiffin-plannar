🚀 Teacher's Setup & Deployment Guide

How do I download this to my computer?

  1. Hover over the generated file container in the right panel.
  2. Click the **Download** icon or copy all of the HTML code.
  3. Save the file as **`index.html`** or **`tiffin_planner.html`** on your desktop.

How can I host & share this with parents?

  • Option A (Easiest): Go to TinyHost, Neocities, or Netlify.
  • Drag & drop this downloaded `.html` file there. They will give you a free, simple link (e.g., msb-tiffin.neocities.org).
  • Send that link to the parents on your class WhatsApp group!

How does the Admin/Rating system work?

  • Syncing Student Names: In Teacher Mode, type any student name inside the date box. It updates on all devices instantly!
  • Viewing Ratings & Reviews: When parents click a day's card, rate it, and submit comment, it goes straight to the "Feedback Wall" in your Teacher Mode.
